Reader's Choice Awards 2024 - Top Picks

Will You Love Me?

By Norhafsah Hamid

Genre: Fiction, Romance

A captivating love story that explores the complexities of relationships, longing, and human connection. A touching read for those who appreciate heartfelt emotion.

Not Here to Stay

By N.F Afrina

Genre: Drama, Fiction

A compelling narrative of a young individual navigating the twists and turns of life, grappling with identity, belonging, and the search for purpose.

The Big Book of Malaysian Horror Stories

By Amir Muhammad

Genre: Horror

A chilling collection of Malaysian horror stories that will keep you up at night, filled with folklore, ghosts, and supernatural events that haunt the mind.

THE GRIMOIRE OF GRAVE FATES

By Hana Alkaf

Genre: Fantasy, Mystery

A dark fantasy about an ancient grimoire that holds the key to uncovering dark secrets, magic, and long-buried fates. A gripping tale for fantasy and mystery lovers.

CHILDREN WE NEVER HAD

By Nadia Khan

Genre: Drama, Family

An emotionally powerful story that explores the themes of loss, regret, and the bittersweet nature of dreams and unfulfilled desires.

Heart of the Sun Warrior

By Tan Sue Lynn

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure

A fierce heroine embarks on an epic quest in a vibrant world of myth and adventure. A perfect pick for readers who love a combination of action and heart.

HADES

By Aishah Zainal

Genre: Fantasy, Mythology

A reimagining of the ancient Greek myth of Hades and Persephone, this novel explores love, power, and betrayal in the depths of the underworld.

THE TINIEST HOUSE OF TIME

By Sreedhevi Lyer

Genre: Science Fiction, Fantasy

A surreal tale where time itself bends and stretches, challenging the way we perceive the world and our place in it. A mind-bending exploration of the concept of time.

HE FOUND YOU LOST, AND GUIDED YOU

By Ayesha Syahira

Genre: Fiction, Spiritual

A deeply moving story that explores themes of faith, redemption, and the guiding presence of love in moments of confusion and despair. A beautifully written book for those seeking hope and inspiration.
